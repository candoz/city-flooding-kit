The project

The project "City Flooding Kit" is in the context of the design of smart environments to support prevention and warning systems for environmental disasters concerning, in particular, river flooding.

The goal is to develop and improve the methodologies and tools for predicting extreme rain events and their impact, focusing on the hydrological response of small streams in urban areas.
